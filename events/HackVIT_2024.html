Event Report

Session 1: The Kickoff

HackVIT 2024 began with an electrifying start as 50 teams from across the region gathered at the Main Auditorium. The opening ceremony featured keynote speeches from industry leaders who emphasized the importance of innovation in solving real-world challenges. The energy was palpable as the problem statements were revealed, setting the stage for 24 hours of intense coding.

Session 2: The Marathon

Throughout the night, the venue buzzed with activity. Mentors from top tech companies circulated among the teams, providing guidance and technical support. From healthcare AI solutions to sustainable tech prototypes, the diversity of ideas was impressive. The "Midnight Coding Session" kept spirits high with energy drinks, music, and impromptu debugging sessions.

Session 3: The Finale

The event concluded with a rigorous judging process where teams pitched their functional prototypes. The panel of judges, comprising experts from academia and industry, praised the technical depth and creativity of the solutions. The closing ceremony celebrated not just the winners, but the spirit of collaboration and learning that defined HackVIT 2024.
