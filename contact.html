Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join ACM?

Any VIT Chennai student can join ACM! Fill out the membership form on our website or visit us during our recruitment drives at the beginning of each semester.

Are events free to attend?

Most of our events are free for ACM members. Some workshops and hackathons may have a nominal registration fee to cover materials and refreshments.

Do I need coding experience?

Not at all! We welcome students of all skill levels. We have beginner-friendly workshops and events designed for those just starting their coding journey.

Can I propose an event?

Absolutely! We encourage member participation. If you have an idea for a workshop, talk, or event, reach out to us through the contact form above.

How often do you meet?

We have regular chapter meetings every two weeks, along with various workshops, seminars, and events throughout the semester. Follow our social media for updates!

What are the benefits?

Members get access to exclusive workshops, networking opportunities, ACM resources, certificates, mentorship, and a supportive community of tech enthusiasts!
