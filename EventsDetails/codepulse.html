Event Logs

CodePulse is a competitive coding event aimed at fostering logical thinking, algorithmic problem-solving, and programming proficiency among participants. The event is structured into multiple rounds, each presenting time-bound challenges that test a participant‚Äôs ability to write efficient and correct code under pressure.

It encourages both individual participation and collaborative team efforts, promoting peer learning and innovation. Participants are free to use programming languages of their choice, including C, C++, Python, Java, and more.

CodePulse not only evaluates technical skills but also cultivates quick thinking and adaptability in a simulated real-world problem-solving environment. Prizes and certificates are awarded to top performers, recognizing their excellence.
